Coffee machine integration app

Want to have a great tasting coffee but you don’t have time to wait for a brewer in the next room to heat up and prepare it for you? That’s where the unique app comes in. With a beautiful Android app you can request a coffee remotely, get notified when it is ready and enjoy your custom flavour preferences.

Bright Inventions implemented the low-level Bluetooth Low Energy communication and achieved the pixel-perfection in applying the customized design provided by the client.

  • Android native application: Bluetooth Low Energy
